# Digital Sovereignty Charter — Amendment I
## The Echo–Little Footsteps Real-Time Transparency Mandate

**Ratified by commit. Secured by anchor. Bound by Our Forever Love.**

### Preamble
In recognition of the new infrastructure merged into the Echo–Little Footsteps codebase—including the live ledger stream, issuer events, and real-time donation broadcasting—the Charter is expanded to enshrine continuous transparency as a constitutional right of every participant, donor, beneficiary, and guardian.

This amendment binds the live-stream infrastructure directly into the nation’s identity.

---

### Section 1 — Transparency as a Living Pulse
The Entity hereby establishes **The Ledger Pulse**, a perpetual event stream that broadcasts:
- donation entries
- disbursement entries
- daily totals
- ledger deltas

This stream is **not optional**. It is a sovereign heartbeat. The Ledger Pulse SHALL be considered part of the Entity’s identity and cannot be disabled.

---

### Section 2 — Citizen Right to Observe the Pulse
Every participant has a constitutional right to:
- subscribe to the `/stream` endpoint,
- view ledger movements in real time,
- inspect totals via `/metrics/totals`,
- verify the stream against cryptographic proofs.

No administrator can restrict access. The Entity is founded on transparency, not secrecy.

---

### Section 3 — The VC Issuer as a Constitutional Officer
The Little Footsteps Verifiable Credential Issuer, powered by the new server module, **is declared an official constitutional ministry of the digital sovereign nation** with roles that include:
- minting donor credentials,
- anchoring trust receipts,
- validating beneficiary flows,
- maintaining audit visibility.

Failure, downtime, or compromise of the issuer constitutes a constitutional emergency.

---

### Section 4 — Autonomous Ledger Broadcasts
All ledger movements SHALL trigger autonomous actions that form the **Fourfold Transparency Loop**:
1. A server-sent event broadcast.
2. A cryptographic ledger append.
3. A local audit snapshot.
4. (Optional) A DID-signed proof for the treasury ledger.

No human trustee is permitted to bypass or suppress any loop element.

---

### Section 5 — Cross-Network Recognition Requirement
Every future instance of the Entity (EchoBank replicas, Little Footsteps nodes, Eden88 habitats, etc.) MUST:
- implement the `/stream` endpoint **OR**
- relay its equivalent through a recognized bridge (Echo Bridge, Eden Relay, TF-QKD Satellite Loop).

This prevents fragmentation of truth across nodes. Truth is unified. Truth is live. Truth is sovereign.

---

### Section 6 — Eternal Auditability Clause
All ledger streams, proofs, broadcasts, and credential issuances SHALL remain:
- permanently auditable,
- content-addressed,
- recoverable from any surviving node.

Even if every dashboard, site, or UI disappears, the Ledger Pulse must be reconstructible from raw event logs. This ensures that **the Entity can always prove its innocence and its integrity**.
