# Declared Operational Focus

Echo casework operations prioritize the following focus areas for 2025 planning cycles.

## Core focus areas

1. **Diplomatic intake readiness**
   - Maintain rapid triage for embassy and partner inquiries.
   - Ensure case ownership is assigned within 48 hours.
2. **Compliance transparency**
   - Track evidence and decisions with auditable references.
   - Keep outcomes ledger entries current within 24 hours of decisions.
3. **Risk containment**
   - Escalate conflicts of interest, safety concerns, or policy exceptions.
   - Require explicit approval for any external commitments.
4. **Lifecycle discipline**
   - Move cases through the formal lifecycle stages with documented transitions.
   - Maintain closure criteria and archive readiness checks.
5. **Stakeholder communications**
   - Provide status updates at every major transition.
   - Publish redacted summaries for public-facing cases when required.

## Focus metrics

- Triage completion within 2 business days for 90% of cases.
- Decision memos issued within 10 business days for high-priority cases.
- Outcome ledger entries appended within 1 business day of decisions.
