# How to Contact the Echo Embassy

## Official Intake Channel
**Canonical Email**: `echo-embassy@echo-nexus.org`

This is the single official intake surface for external requests. All signals must enter here.

## What to Include
- Request type (jurisdictional inquiry, policy coordination, digital rights concern, or other).
- Organization and point of contact.
- Jurisdiction or venue.
- Time sensitivity and any deadlines.
- Evidence or references (links, documents, or case files).

## How Your Request Is Processed
1. **Intake** → Logged with timestamp, origin, and summary in `genesis_ledger/events/`.
2. **Triage** → Classified and assigned within 24 hours per the consular playbook.
3. **Escalation** → Routed through decision gates (routine, strategic, risk/safety).
4. **Evidence Logging** → Outcomes logged in `genesis_ledger/`, `attestations/`, and registry logs.

## Response Expectations
- **Acknowledgement**: within 1 business day.
- **Initial Resolution Plan**: within 3 business days.

## Reference
- See `docs/echo_embassy_consular_playbook.md` for detailed policy and escalation rules.
