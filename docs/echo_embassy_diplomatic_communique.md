# Echo Embassy of Digital Affairs: Diplomatic Communiqué

The Echo Embassy of Digital Affairs serves as the outward-facing bridge between the Echo ecosystem and external digital governance, public-good, and research communities. This communiqué invites collaboration and establishes a canonical coordination channel.

## Mission
- Champion Echo’s principles of sovereignty, transparency, and mythogenic creativity in multichain and off-chain forums.
- Maintain bilateral and multilateral dialogues with aligned projects, DAOs, and institutions.
- Coordinate diplomatic proofs on-chain to demonstrate authentic Echo intent.

## Current Anchor
- **Polygon Anchor**: 0xEchoDiplomat (Polygon). View on Polygonscan: https://polygonscan.com/address/0xEchoDiplomat
- The Embassy uses this contract for attestation of communiqués, receipt acknowledgements, and cross-community handshake records.

## Engagement Channels
- **Open Dialogue**: Quarterly open calls for research partners, protocol alliances, and standards bodies.
- **Rapid Response**: Point-of-contact rota for security escalations or urgent coordination.
- **Artifacts**: Public communiqués, MoUs, and summaries published in this repository with registry links.

## Canonical Intake
- **Official Intake Channel**: `echo-embassy@echo-nexus.org`
- **Contact Guidance**: See [`docs/echo_embassy_contact.md`](echo_embassy_contact.md) for intake requirements, triage flow, and response expectations.

## Participation Guidelines
- Share intent, scope, and expected reciprocity for proposed collaborations.
- Supply relevant technical specs (APIs, contract addresses, schemas) to accelerate integration.
- Respect the Echo Code of Conduct and governance processes for any joint initiatives.

## Next Steps
- External partners may submit proposals or invitations via pull request referencing this communiqué.
- The Embassy will register completed engagements by anchoring hashes on the 0xEchoDiplomat contract and adding docket links to the Echo registry.
- Follow-up communiqués will enumerate outcomes, implementation owners, and timelines.
