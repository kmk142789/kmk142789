# Wyoming Digital Entity Pathways

## Framework Snapshot
| Framework | Category | Key Digital Capabilities | Supervising Authority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Wyoming LLC | Corporate | Tokenized membership interests; designation as Special Purpose Depository Institution (SPDI) affiliate | Wyoming Secretary of State; Wyoming Division of Banking |
| Wyoming Nonprofit Corporation | Nonprofit | Grantmaking and governance token administration without equity issuance | Wyoming Secretary of State |
| Wyoming DAO LLC | DAO | Smart contract articles; on-chain governance recognition; limited liability for token holders | Wyoming Secretary of State |

## Procedural Requirements

### Wyoming Limited Liability Company (LLC)
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (USD) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Name availability search via Wyoming Secretary of State portal | $0 | Same day | Reserve name if desired before filing. |
| 2 | Articles of Organization (online or mail) | $100 filing fee | Immediate for online; 2-3 weeks by mail | Include optional statement electing SPDI affiliation or virtual asset purpose. |
| 3 | Initial Resolution adopting operating agreement | Internal | Within 30 days | Operating agreement can reference smart contracts for governance. |
| 4 | Beneficial Ownership Information (BOI) filing with FinCEN | $0 | Within 30 days of formation | Required for most entities under Corporate Transparency Act starting 2024. |
| 5 | Annual Report filing | $60 minimum (based on assets) | Due on anniversary month | Allows amendment to designate registered agent and digital asset notices. |

### Wyoming Nonprofit Corporation
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (USD) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Draft Articles of Incorporation (nonprofit) | $25 filing fee | 3-5 business days | Purpose statement can reference digital public goods. |
| 2 | Adopt Bylaws referencing token governance policies | Internal | Within 30 days | Include conflict-of-interest policy to satisfy IRS expectations. |
| 3 | File IRS Form 1023/1024 (if seeking federal tax exemption) | $600 (1023) / $275 (1023-EZ) | 3-6 months | Align token issuance model with IRS charitable rules. |
| 4 | Register for state charitable solicitation (if fundraising) | Varies | Prior to public fundraising | Coordinate multi-state registrations if nationwide campaigns are planned. |
| 5 | Annual Report + optional charitable renewals | $25 | Anniversary month | Maintain separate ledger for token treasury. |

### Wyoming DAO LLC
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (USD) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Organize DAO LLC via Secretary of State portal | $100 filing fee | Instant online | Articles must identify underlying smart contract and publicly available identifier. |
| 2 | Draft smart contract-linked operating agreement | Internal | Prior to token launch | Must define quorum, proposal thresholds, and emergency upgrades. |
| 3 | File updates when smart contract changes materially | $60 amendment fee | Within 30 days of change | Required to maintain recognition of on-chain governance. |
| 4 | Maintain registered agent & Wyoming principal office | $50-150 annually | Ongoing | Many DAOs use compliance service providers for local presence. |
| 5 | Annual Report confirming DAO status | $60 | Anniversary month | Report must restate smart contract address(es) in force. |

## Timeline Considerations
* LLC or DAO LLC can be live within 1 business day when filing online.
* Nonprofit IRS determinations take the longest; plan for 3-6 months before receiving exemption.
* SPDI applications are separate banking charters and add 9-12 months if pursued.
