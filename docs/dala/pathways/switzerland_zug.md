# Zug (Switzerland) Digital Entity Pathways

## Framework Snapshot
| Framework | Category | Key Digital Capabilities | Supervising Authority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Swiss GmbH | Corporate | Tokenized participation certificates; FINMA-compliant custody licensing | Zug Commercial Register; FINMA |
| Swiss Association | Nonprofit | Member-token governance; open-source stewardship | Zug Commercial Register |
| Swiss Foundation (Stiftung) | DAO / Foundation | Protocol IP custody; treasury endowment management | Federal Supervisory Authority for Foundations; FINMA |

## Procedural Requirements

### Swiss GmbH
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (CHF) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Draft articles of association (including token clauses) | Legal fees vary | 1-2 weeks | Minimum capital CHF 20,000 (can be in-kind via token valuation if approved). |
| 2 | Public notary deed of incorporation | ~CHF 600-1,200 | Same day | Incorporators sign before notary; can appoint digital asset-savvy directors. |
| 3 | Bank confirmation of capital contribution | Bank fees vary | 1-3 days | Crypto contributions require valuation report and AML review. |
| 4 | File with Zug Commercial Register | CHF 600 | 5-10 business days | Provide beneficial owner declaration and UBO register. |
| 5 | Register with VAT (if applicable) and obtain FINMA guidance | 0-CHF 150 | 2-4 weeks | Required if offering virtual asset services or payment tokens. |

### Swiss Association
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (CHF) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Draft statutes with member token governance | Internal | 1 week | At least two members; no minimum capital. |
| 2 | Constitutive assembly minutes | Internal | Day of formation | Capture adoption of digital governance policies. |
| 3 | Register in Commercial Register (optional but recommended) | CHF 120 | 10-15 business days | Mandatory if conducting commercial activity or subject to audit. |
| 4 | AML registration (if handling virtual assets) | CHF 300-2,000 | 2-4 weeks | Join SRO (e.g., VQF) for compliance oversight. |
| 5 | Annual member meeting and audit (if thresholds met) | Audit fees vary | Annual | Maintain transparency on treasury and protocol upgrades. |

### Swiss Foundation (Stiftung)
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (CHF) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Draft foundation charter & regulations | Legal fees vary | 3-4 weeks | Must specify irrevocable purpose and governance token policy. |
| 2 | Notarization and capital endowment (min CHF 50,000) | CHF 1,000-2,000 | Same day | Endowment can include crypto assets with valuation report. |
| 3 | Submit to Federal Supervisory Authority for Foundations | Included | 6-12 weeks | Provide business plan, AML controls, and protocol oversight documentation. |
| 4 | Register with Commercial Register of Zug | CHF 600 | 2-3 weeks | Required for legal personality. |
| 5 | Annual reporting & audit | Audit fees vary | Annual | FINMA oversight triggered if issuing payment or utility tokens publicly. |
