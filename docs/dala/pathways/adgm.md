# Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) Digital Entity Pathways

## Framework Snapshot
| Framework | Category | Key Digital Capabilities | Supervising Authority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ADGM Private Company Limited by Shares | Corporate | Access to FSRA virtual asset permissions; English-law corporate governance | ADGM Registration Authority; Financial Services Regulatory Authority (FSRA) |
| ADGM Foundation | Nonprofit / Foundation | Ring-fenced treasury management; philanthropic grant programs | ADGM Registration Authority |
| ADGM DLT Foundation | DAO | Recognized token-holder governance; smart contract-referenced constitution | ADGM Registration Authority; FSRA |

## Procedural Requirements

### ADGM Private Company Limited by Shares
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (AED) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Reserve company name | 200 | 1 day | Valid for 90 days. |
| 2 | Submit incorporation application (memorandum, articles, business plan) | 1,500 incorporation fee + 10,000 commercial license | 5-10 business days | Include virtual asset activities in business plan for FSRA review. |
| 3 | Lease registered office within ADGM | Varies | Prior to license issuance | Flexi-desk solutions available for startups. |
| 4 | Apply for FSRA financial services permissions (if providing VASP services) | 5,000 application + supervision fees | 4-6 months | Requires AML compliance officer, risk framework, and technology governance. |
| 5 | Annual return & license renewal | 4,000 | Annual | Provide audited financials if exceeding threshold. |

### ADGM Foundation
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (AED) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Draft charter and by-laws | Professional fees vary | 1-2 weeks | Define purposes, guardians, and governance arrangements for token treasury. |
| 2 | Submit foundation application | 1,000 registration + 200 name reservation | 5-7 business days | No minimum capital; must appoint council and guardian. |
| 3 | File ultimate beneficial owner (UBO) declaration | Included | Concurrent | Identify settlor, beneficiaries, and controllers. |
| 4 | Maintain accounting records within ADGM | Professional fees vary | Ongoing | Required even when assets are digital. |
| 5 | Annual renewal | 200 | Annual | Update council/guardian details and confirm purpose compliance. |

### ADGM DLT Foundation
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (AED) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Prepare DLT foundation charter referencing protocol smart contracts | Professional fees vary | 2-3 weeks | Charter must list primary smart contract addresses and governance modules. |
| 2 | Submit application under DLT Foundations Regulations 2023 | 5,000 | 10-15 business days | Provide tokenomics paper, compliance policies, and guardian details. |
| 3 | Register responsible individuals (council, guardian, resident agent) | Included | Concurrent | At least one council member or guardian must be ADGM-resident. |
| 4 | File AML/CTF compliance manual with FSRA | Advisory fees vary | 1-2 months | Required if handling virtual assets or treasury operations. |
| 5 | Annual supervisory return & technology attestation | 2,000 | Annual | Report smart contract upgrades, treasury balances, and validator operations. |

## Timeline Considerations
* Baseline company or foundation set-up completes in ~2 weeks; obtaining FSRA permissions extends to 4-6 months.
* DLT Foundations offer fastest DAO recognition in-region but require ongoing supervisory reporting.
