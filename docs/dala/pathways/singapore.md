# Singapore Digital Entity Pathways

## Framework Snapshot
| Framework | Category | Key Digital Capabilities | Supervising Authority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Private Company Limited by Shares (Pte. Ltd.) | Corporate | MAS licensing for digital payment token services; tokenized equity | Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority (ACRA); Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) |
| Company Limited by Guarantee (CLG) | Nonprofit | Ecosystem grant management; governance token stewardship without share capital | ACRA; Commissioner of Charities |
| Variable Capital Company (VCC) | DAO / Fund Structure | Tokenized fund shares; segregated sub-funds for DeFi strategies | ACRA (VCC Office); MAS |

## Procedural Requirements

### Private Company Limited by Shares (Pte. Ltd.)
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (SGD) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Name application via BizFile+ | $15 | Instant if no referrals | Include "digital asset" or "technology" keywords as needed. |
| 2 | Incorporation filing (constitution, shareholder/ director particulars) | $300 | Same day once approved | Constitution can incorporate token governance schedules. |
| 3 | Appoint company secretary and local director | Professional fees vary | Within 6 months (secretary) / immediate (director) | At least one director must be ordinarily resident in Singapore. |
| 4 | Register under Payment Services Act (if offering DPT services) | $1,000-10,000 application fee depending on license class | 6-12 months | Prepare compliance manuals addressing AML/CFT and technology risk. |
| 5 | Annual Return filing + corporate tax | $60 AR filing fee | AR within 7 months of FY end; tax 3 months later | Maintain XBRL financial statements including token treasury valuations. |

### Company Limited by Guarantee (CLG)
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (SGD) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Name application | $15 | Same day | Ensure suffix "Limited" unless exempted. |
| 2 | Incorporation with guarantee clause (BizFile+) | $300 | 1-2 days | Draft constitution specifying nonprofit objectives and digital governance. |
| 3 | Register as Charity (optional) | $0 | 3-6 months | Requires detailed business plan, budget, and governance policies. |
| 4 | Apply for Institutions of a Public Character (IPC) status (if needed) | $0 | 3-9 months | Enables tax-deductible donations; rigorous compliance. |
| 5 | Annual filing: AR, financial statements, charity returns | $60 AR fee | Annual | Use digital asset accounting policies aligning with Charities Accounting Standard. |

### Variable Capital Company (VCC)
| Step | Documents & Filings | Fees (SGD) | Timeline | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Name reservation via VCC portal | $15 | Same day | Valid for 120 days. |
| 2 | VCC incorporation (constitution, manager appointment) | $8,000 | 2-3 weeks | Must appoint MAS-regulated fund manager or rely on umbrella arrangement. |
| 3 | Register sub-funds with MAS | Included | Concurrent with incorporation | Each sub-fund can map to specific DAO vault or strategy. |
| 4 | File AML/CFT policies & risk assessments | Professional fees vary | Prior to operations | Align with MAS Notice VCC-N01 requirements for digital assets. |
| 5 | Annual return & financial statements | $200 | Within 6 months of FY end | Provide NAV statements for each tokenized sub-fund. |
