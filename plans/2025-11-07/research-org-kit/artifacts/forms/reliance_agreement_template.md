# Draft IRB Reliance Agreement

This Reliance Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Echo Labs Cooperative** ("Relying Organization") and **Bay Area Collaborative IRB** ("Reviewing IRB").

## Purpose
To outline responsibilities when the Reviewing IRB serves as the IRB of record for human subjects research conducted by Echo.

## Key Terms
1. **Scope:** Applies to studies listed in Exhibit A (initially "Community Credential Adoption Study").
2. **Regulatory Compliance:** Parties agree to comply with 45 CFR 46 and applicable HIPAA provisions.
3. **Responsibilities:**
   - Reviewing IRB conducts initial and continuing review, reports determinations to Echo.
   - Echo ensures investigators complete human subjects training and comply with IRB determinations.
4. **Reporting:** Echo reports unanticipated problems to the Reviewing IRB within 5 business days and to OHRP as required.
5. **Data Protection:** Echo maintains ISO/IEC 27557-aligned safeguards and documents them in the Data Security SOP.
6. **Term & Termination:** Agreement effective upon signatures; either party may terminate with 30 days' notice.
7. **Digital Signatures:** DID-based signatures with timestamped ledger entries accepted as legally binding.
