# Human Subjects Protection Plan

## Ethical Framework
Echo adheres to the Belmont Report principles—Respect for Persons, Beneficence, and Justice—and codifies them within investigator training modules.

## IRB Engagement
- All human subjects research must secure written IRB approval prior to recruitment.
- Modifications require prior IRB approval; emergency changes reported within 5 business days.

## Informed Consent
- Consent forms written at ≤ 8th-grade reading level.
- Participants receive digital copies via secure portal plus option for paper.
- Re-consent triggered for any protocol changes affecting risk.

## Privacy & Confidentiality
- Data classified per ISO/IEC 27557 with controls matched to risk tiers.
- Identifiers stored separately with tokenization; access logged via immutable audit trail.

## Training Requirements
- Investigators complete CITI training; refresher every 3 years.
- Echo issues W3C VCs documenting training completion linked to investigator DIDs.

## Reporting & Oversight
- Adverse events escalated to Research Compliance Officer immediately.
- Annual continuing review submissions compiled 45 days before IRB expiration.
