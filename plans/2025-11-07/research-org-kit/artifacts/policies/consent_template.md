# Informed Consent Template – Community Credential Adoption Study

## Study Overview
You are invited to participate in a research study about community adoption of digital credentials conducted by Echo Labs Cooperative.

## Procedures
- 60-minute virtual interview recorded with your permission.
- Optional follow-up survey (15 minutes).

## Risks & Discomforts
- Minimal risk; potential discomfort discussing technology adoption challenges.

## Benefits
- Contribution to design of inclusive credentialing systems.
- Access to study findings summary.

## Confidentiality
- Interviews transcribed and de-identified.
- Data stored on encrypted servers; only research team has access.
- Results reported in aggregate; no personal identifiers shared.

## Compensation
- Participants receive a CAD 50 digital stipend issued as a W3C VC redeemable via partnered platform.

## Voluntary Participation
- Participation is voluntary; you may withdraw at any time without penalty.
