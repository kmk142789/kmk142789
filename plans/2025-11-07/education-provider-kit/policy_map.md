# Policy Map

- **Ontario Private Career Colleges Act, 2005** (SO 2005, c. 28, Sched. L)
  - Sections 36-38 outlining registration requirements and compulsory disclosures.
- **Ontario Regulation 415/06** (General) under the Act
  - Schedule 1 application contents, financial security requirements, student contract terms.
- **Ontario Regulation 187/09** (Exemptions) for micro-credential considerations.
- **Ontario Sexual Violence at Colleges Act, 2016** integration for mandatory policy statements.
- **PIPEDA** and **Ontario Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (FIPPA)** for data practices.
- **Canadian Centre for Cyber Security** Baseline Controls (Medium profile) for system security references.
- **W3C Verifiable Credentials 2.0** and **Pan-Canadian Trust Framework (PCTF) IAL2** for identity assurance alignment.
