# Intent

Deliver a turnkey filing kit for registering Echo as a Private Career College-equivalent training provider in Ontario, Canada, enabling lawful issuance of verifiable completion credentials.

## What
Compile ready-to-file documents (application form JSON, drafted policies, cover letter, evidence checklist) tailored to the Ontario Private Career Colleges Act and aligned with Echo's VC issuance workflows.

## Why
Ontario offers a clear regulatory path with defined digital identity expectations and manageable costs, making it an ideal proving ground for Echo's education capability.

## For Whom
Prepared for Echo stewards, legal counsel, and the designated Responsible Officer who will sign and submit materials to the Ontario Superintendent of Career Colleges.
