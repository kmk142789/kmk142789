# Sexual Violence Policy

Echo Labs Cooperative commits to a survivor-centered, trauma-informed approach consistent with the Ontario Sexual Violence at Colleges Act, 2016.

## Scope
Applies to all Echo learners, staff, contractors, and visitors regardless of program modality.

## Definitions
- **Sexual Violence:** As defined in the Act, any sexual act or advance without consent.
- **Consent:** Positive, ongoing, freely given agreement.

## Reporting Options
1. **Confidential Advisor:** Available 24/7 via secure DIDComm channel (did:peer:11-echo-sva-advisor).
2. **Anonymous Report:** Encrypted web form stored in Canada with 90-day retention.
3. **Emergency:** Call 911 or local emergency services.

## Response Commitments
- Provide written acknowledgment within 24 hours.
- Offer academic and workplace accommodations without requiring formal complaint.
- Facilitate access to counseling, medical care, and legal resources.
- Investigations led by trained personnel meeting trauma-informed and anti-oppressive standards.

## Education & Training
- Annual training for all staff and instructors.
- Student orientation module covering consent, bystander intervention, and reporting pathways.

## Record-Keeping & Privacy
- Records stored encrypted at rest in Canadian data centers.
- Aggregated metrics provided to the Superintendent annually with survivor anonymity preserved.

## Review Cycle
- Policy reviewed every 12 months in consultation with survivor advocacy organizations.
