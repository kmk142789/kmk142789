# Accessibility Support Plan

Echo commits to meeting Ontario Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA) standards.

## Accommodation Process
1. Learner submits request via accessible portal (WCAG 2.2 AA compliant).
2. Accessibility Coordinator responds within 3 business days.
3. Individualized accommodation plan documented and shared securely.

## Available Supports
- Captioned and transcripted recordings for all online sessions.
- Adjustable seating and assistive technology in physical spaces.
- Flexible assessment windows with alternative formats.

## Training & Review
- Annual AODA training for all staff.
- Quarterly review of accommodation effectiveness with learner feedback loops.
