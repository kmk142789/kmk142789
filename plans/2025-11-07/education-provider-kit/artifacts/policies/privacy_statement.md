# Privacy Statement

Echo Labs Cooperative complies with PIPEDA, FIPPA (where applicable), and the Pan-Canadian Trust Framework.

## Collection
We collect personal information necessary for admission, delivery of training, issuance of verifiable credentials, and regulatory reporting. Collection is limited to lawful and reasonable purposes.

## Use & Disclosure
- Credential issuance relies on W3C Verifiable Credentials 2.0 with StatusList2021 revocation.
- Personal information may be disclosed to the Superintendent of Career Colleges, Ontario Student Assistance Program, and approved auditors when required.
- Cross-border transfers follow contractual clauses ensuring equivalency of protection.

## Safeguards
- Identity proofing at PCTF IAL2.
- Multi-factor authentication and encryption at rest using AES-256.
- Annual penetration testing; incident response within 72 hours.

## Access & Correction
Learners can request access or corrections via privacy@echo.example; responses within 30 days per PIPEDA timelines.

## Retention & Disposal
Student records retained for 7 years after graduation or withdrawal, then securely destroyed using NIST SP 800-88 rev.1 techniques.
