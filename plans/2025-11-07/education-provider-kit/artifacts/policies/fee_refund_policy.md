# Fee Refund Policy

This policy aligns with Section 25 of the Private Career Colleges Act, 2005 and Ontario Regulation 415/06.

| Scenario | Refund Calculation | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Student withdraws ≥ 14 days before start date | 100% tuition and ancillary fees | Admin fee capped at CAD 150 |
| Student withdraws < 14 days before start date but before instruction begins | 100% tuition, 50% ancillary fees | Materials not returned deducted at cost |
| Student withdraws after instruction begins (≤ 25% of program) | Tuition refunded proportionally to hours not delivered; ancillary fees pro-rated | Calculation: tuition_paid * (1 - hours_delivered / total_hours) |
| Program cancelled by Echo | 100% of all fees | Refund within 30 days |
| Student dismissed for cause | Refunds calculated per above unless misconduct involves fraud | Documentation retained in student file |

Refunds are issued from the designated trust account via EFT within 14 days of eligibility confirmation. Records of refunds are retained for 7 years.
