# Policy Map

- **United States (federal + CA/NY references)**
  - Higher Education Opportunity Act (20 U.S.C. §1001 et seq.) for education/training recognition.
  - National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) SP 800-63 for digital identity assurance.
  - Uniform Law Commission's Revised Uniform Law on Notarial Acts (RULONA) for remote online notarization.
- **Canada (federal + Ontario)**
  - Ontario Private Career Colleges Act, 2005 for vocational training providers.
  - Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) for credential data handling.
  - Ontario Electronic Commerce Act, 2000 for electronic attestations.
- **European Union (Germany focal point)**
  - EU eIDAS Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 for trust services and qualified attestations.
  - GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) Articles 5, 6, 30 for data governance.
  - German Weiterbildungsförderung regulations for continuing education providers (SGB III).
- **Singapore**
  - Private Education Act and Committee for Private Education (CPE) registration framework.
  - Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) for credential issuance.
  - Evidence Act (Chapter 97) digital signatures provisions.
- **Kenya**
  - TVET Act, 2013 for training and skill certification centers.
  - Data Protection Act, 2019 and subsidiary regulations for VC storage.
  - Land Registration Act, 2012 for property record verification pathways.

Cross-cutting standards:
- W3C Verifiable Credentials Data Model 2.0.
- ISO/IEC 18013-5 (mobile IDs) and ISO/IEC 27001 controls for security posture.
- Trust-over-IP (ToIP) interoperability profiles for multi-jurisdiction acceptance.
