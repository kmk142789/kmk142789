# Policy Map

- **Land Registration Act, 2012 (Kenya)** – Sections 6, 9, 14 governing maintenance of land register and duties of Chief Land Registrar.
- **Land Regulations, 2017** – Provisions on electronic transactions and approved intermediaries.
- **Kenya Information and Communications Act** – Electronic certification services framework.
- **Data Protection Act, 2019** and **Data Protection (General) Regulations, 2021** – Data handling, registration with ODPC.
- **Anti-Corruption and Economic Crimes Act, 2003** – Integrity requirements for government partners.
- **ISACA Cobit 2019** + **ISO/IEC 27001** controls referenced in security dossier.
- **W3C Verifiable Credentials 2.0** with **OGC Land Administration Domain Model (LADM)** mapping for property credentials.
