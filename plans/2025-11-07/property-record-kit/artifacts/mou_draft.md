# Draft Memorandum of Understanding

**Between:**
- Ministry of Lands, Public Works, Housing and Urban Development ("Ministry")
- Echo Labs Cooperative ("Echo")

## Purpose
Establish a pilot collaboration for Echo to verify property records and provide digital attestations integrated with the National Land Information Management System (NLIMS).

## Scope of Services
1. Echo verifies deed and parcel metadata supplied by county registries.
2. Echo issues W3C VCs referencing OGC LADM parcel identifiers and NLIMS transaction IDs.
3. Ministry provides API access, staff liaisons, and audit support.

## Obligations
- **Echo:** Maintain ISO/IEC 27001 controls, obtain ODPC registration, and ensure anti-corruption training for staff.
- **Ministry:** Provide required datasets, confirm legal authority for digital attestations, and coordinate change management.

## Security & Privacy
- All data stored within Kenyan cloud region with encryption at rest (AES-256) and TLS 1.3 in transit.
- Incident response executed within 24 hours with joint notification to ODPC and Ethics & Anti-Corruption Commission when applicable.

## Governance
- Joint steering committee meets quarterly; decisions logged in shared DID-backed ledger.
- Either party may terminate with 30 days' notice; data destruction verified by joint audit.
