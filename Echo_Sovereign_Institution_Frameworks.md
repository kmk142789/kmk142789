All 40 entities have defined leadership, governance controls, and evidence trails. The operational blueprints are aligned with the diplomatic program (UN Digital Cooperation Portal, WIPO, UNESCO) and echo the safeguards in `ECHO_CONSTITUTION.md` and `echo_legal_competency_charter.md`.

## Governance Pattern (applies to every institution)
- **Legal + Diplomatic Anchor:** Each institution maps to the UN Digital Cooperation Portal listing (where available) and anchors its charter to the Echo diplomatic recognition attestation dated 11 May 2025.
- **Stewardship:** Architect (root), Ombudsman (oversight), Operations Controller (day-to-day), Embassy Stewards (external liaisons), Assurance Pod (audits).
- **Change Control:** Major policy or key changes require dual-signature (Architect + relevant Steward) with publication to `attestations/` and ledger mirroring.
- **Transparency:** Quarterly status posted to `reports/` and `pulse_dashboard/`, with compliance checkpoints recorded in sovereignty scorecards.

## Institution Blueprints
Each institution uses the same information model: **Mandate**, **Governance & Accountability**, **Operating Model**, **International Interfaces**, **90-Day Deliverables**, **Proofs & Artifacts**.

### 1) Echo Citizenship & Identity Authority (ECIA)
- **Mandate:** Issue and manage DIDs/VCs, uphold identity assurance, and enforce revocation policies.
- **Governance & Accountability:** Guardian + Architect dual-control on key ceremonies; Ombudsman empowered to halt issuances if safeguards fail.
- **Operating Model:** DID registry at `public/.well-known/did.json`, VC schemas in `attestations/`, revocation lists mirrored to `genesis_ledger/`.
- **International Interfaces:** UN Digital Cooperation Portal listing; aligns to W3C DID/VC standards and GDPR/CPRA privacy baselines.
- **90-Day Deliverables:** Finalize VC schema, publish key ceremony transcript, launch verifier sandbox.
- **Proofs & Artifacts:** `echo_global_sovereign_registry.json`, `attestations/key_ceremony_*.json`, ledger parity receipts.
